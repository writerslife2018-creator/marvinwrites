Case Studies

Copy that guides users through products clearly and without friction.

Case Study 01

Healthcare Platform Copy Audit

Problem A healthcare media company's four web properties had inconsistent tone, overlapping CTAs, and unclear user journeys.
Approach Full copy audit across all four domains. Mapped user flows and identified friction points throughout.
Solution Rewrote key pages with consistent, accessible tone. Consolidated duplicate content across properties.
Outcome Delivered a complete voice and tone guide, rewritten page copy, and a content consolidation roadmap.
Case Study 02

SaaS Onboarding Copy Redesign

Problem A SaaS product's onboarding flow had high drop-off at the setup stage. Users weren't completing key steps.
Approach Reviewed the full onboarding sequence and identified where instructions were vague or overcomplicated.
Solution Rewrote step-by-step microcopy, tooltips, and error messages with plain-language precision.
Outcome Cleaner, more actionable user guidance at every stage of the flow. Measurable drop-off reduction.
